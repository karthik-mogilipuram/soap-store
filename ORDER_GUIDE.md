# Order Guide — for whoever answers the WhatsApp

You don't need to know anything about websites to run this. Customers browse the page, tap buttons, and the site writes their order message for them. Your job starts when that message lands on WhatsApp (+91-9396857360).

## What a customer sees

1. They open https://karthik-mogilipuram.github.io/soap-store/ on their phone.
2. They tap **Add to order** on the soaps they want (and **+** / **−** to change quantity).
3. A green bar appears at the bottom showing their total. The site does the math: **₹149 per bar, any 3 bars for ₹399** — automatically. 4 bars = ₹548 (one ₹399 bundle + one ₹149 bar), 6 bars = ₹798, 7 bars = ₹947.
4. They tap **Send order on WhatsApp**. WhatsApp opens with a ready-made message.

## What you receive

A WhatsApp message like this:

```
Hi! I'd like to order:
2× Neem
1× Charcoal

Total: ₹399
Code: AMMA20
```

The total in the message is already correct — the site calculated it.

## What you do

1. **Reply to confirm** the order and ask for their delivery address.
2. **Tell them to pay by UPI** using the QR code on the page (they've already seen it) and to send you a payment screenshot.
3. **Check the screenshot**, then confirm the order is placed and tell them when to expect delivery.

That's the whole flow. If someone messages without the ready-made format ("do you have neem soap?"), just chat normally — the same prices apply.

## The promo code

`AMMA20` in the message just tells you they came from the website. The bundle pricing is already in their total — you don't need to discount anything further unless you want to.
